FlowMap leverages AI to automate the process of mapping workflows and matching customer feedback to pain points. This saves time and ensures accurate, actionable insights throughout the sales and onboarding cycles.

FlowMap is built for:

  • Sales Engineers who need to visualize and communicate complex solutions.
  • Sales Leaders looking to streamline processes and prove ROI faster.

We want to know how well FlowMap fits into your workflow, what features you'd like to see, and any challenges you encounter while using the tool. Your feedback will directly influence future updates and enhancements.

Yes! FlowMap enables you to collect and visualize feedback from multiple sources, ensuring that all pain points are addressed and workflows are aligned across teams.

FlowMap includes tools to quantify the value of process improvements by showcasing measurable outcomes like time savings, cost reductions, and efficiency gains. These insights can be presented directly to decision-makers.

Can I use FlowMap for onboarding?

Absolutely! FlowMap ensures a seamless handoff from sales to onboarding by providing clear, actionable workflows tailored to your customers' needs.

Is FlowMap difficult to learn?

Not at all! FlowMap's intuitive drag-and-drop interface is designed to be simple to use, even for first-time users. You can start creating workflows within minutes.

What industries does FlowMap support?

FlowMap is ideal for industries with complex sales and onboarding processes, including SaaS, manufacturing, consulting, and enterprise solutions.
